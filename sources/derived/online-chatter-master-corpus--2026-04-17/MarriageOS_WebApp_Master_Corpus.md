# MarriageOS — Master Corpus Rebuild V3

This rebuild is a **contribution-level master corpus**, not a fresh scrape of the original Reddit/X/PDF source universe.

It was rebuilt from the uploaded swipe-file extracts with four rules:

1. **Preserve the full per-clip schema**
2. **Keep attention-bearing and risky material as tagged raw material**
3. **Deduplicate only at the semantic-family level**
4. **Note reinforcement when multiple uploads carried the same truth, hook, or pattern**

## How this rebuild handles duplicates

Two items were treated as duplicates when they expressed the same underlying truth, pattern, or hook, even if the wording differed.

When that happened, this corpus keeps:
- the **sharpest raw clip**
- the **cleanest adaptation / duplicate variant**
- the **merged strongest tags**
- a **reinforcement note** showing where the idea recurred across the uploaded contributions

## Schema used for each corpus record

- RAW CLIP
- CLEANEST ADAPTATION / DUPLICATE VARIANT KEPT WITH FAMILY (when relevant)
- MODE
- CLIP TYPE
- WHY IT WORKS
- RISK FLAG
- KEEP / MAYBE / DISCARD
- THEME TAGS
- SOCIAL FUEL ASSESSMENT
- REINFORCEMENT NOTE
- DEDUPE NOTE (when useful)
