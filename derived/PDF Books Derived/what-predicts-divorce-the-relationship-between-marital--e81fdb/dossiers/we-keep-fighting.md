## Core content logic
- The couple is not just 'communicating badly'; they are caught in a repeating loop.
- The real danger is not disagreement alone but the speed with which negativity starts feeding more negativity.
- Flooding reduces the ability to hear repair, think clearly, and stay soft.
- Stonewalling is often the point where one partner is too overactivated or too shut down to continue well.
- First-line intervention should focus on slowing the loop, naming the escalation markers, and making repair audible again.
