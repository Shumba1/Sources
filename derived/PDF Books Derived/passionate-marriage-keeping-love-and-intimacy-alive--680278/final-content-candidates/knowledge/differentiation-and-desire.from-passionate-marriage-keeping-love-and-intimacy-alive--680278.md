# Differentiation And Desire

## Summary
This article explains a recurring relationship mechanism and translates it into practical next steps aligned to a repair-first model.

## Section 1 — The pattern
Couples often misread intent under pressure and then react to the misread rather than to the actual signal.

## Section 2 — Why it compounds
Unchallenged certainty and defensive tone create a fast loop: trigger, assumption, escalation, withdrawal.

## Section 3 — What interrupts it
The fastest interrupt is naming the loop and reducing stakes before problem-solving.

## What this means in real life
You need a short, repeatable reset move that works under stress, not a perfect conversation.
