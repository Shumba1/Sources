# Source summary

A broad relationship book organised around eight guided conversations:
trust and commitment, conflict, sex and intimacy, work and money, family,
fun and adventure, growth and spirituality, and dreams.

For MarriageOS, the strongest value is not the “eight dates” wrapper.
The strongest value is the underlying mechanism layer:
small trust-building actions, conflict repair, meaning-level money conversations,
desire-supporting rituals outside the bedroom, post-baby strain logic,
play/novelty as anti-drift behaviour, and dream-level interpretation beneath conflict.
