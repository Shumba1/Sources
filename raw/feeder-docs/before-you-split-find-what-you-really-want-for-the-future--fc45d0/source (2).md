# Source summary

*Before You Split* is a decision-stage marriage book aimed at people who feel
close to giving up. The strongest MarriageOS value is not the author's
persuasion to stay married. The strongest value is the mechanism layer:
distinguishing unhappy from harmful, slowing decision panic, exposing blame
stories, validating emotion, reducing destructive conflict, protecting children,
rebuilding support, and recovering fun/intimacy through low-friction moves.

# Editor notes

Best fit:
- repair modules for “I’m done”, fighting loops, and child-impact conflict
- knowledge support for unhappy vs harmful
- support tools for forgiveness, support networks, and intimacy recovery

Use with caution:
- Christian language and spiritual directives
- divorce-comparison rhetoric and separation regret framing
- any advice that could be read as “stay no matter what”
- broad claims about emotional abuse being overused

Reject as canonical posture:
- moral pressure to preserve marriage at all costs
- minimising abusive or harmful environments
- long-form workbook tone as primary UX
